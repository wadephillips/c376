Opie vs. Ollie-thra

In an attempt to quell the on going rampage of Opie, Destroyer of Worlds, the U.N. authorized the summoning of Ollie-thra, and an epic battle ensued. In the waters of Sydney Harbor, Ollie-thra struck at Opie, Destroyer of Worlds. As these two ferocious beasts struggled for dominance, stray laser beams and gouts of flame counsumed much of the waterfront.

Ultimately, the Battle of Sydney was a draw. The fearsome struggle damaged each creature, and they were forced to retreat and lick their wounds. Opie, Destryer of Worlds limped into the Australian Outback and vanished. Ollie-thra flew back to his cocoon, high atop a volcano on a remote South Pacific island. For now the world waits and wonders if there will be another meeting of these two titans.
