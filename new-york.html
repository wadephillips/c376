Opie Destroys New York City.

Opie's attack of New York City was epic and devestating. He left the city in absolute shambles. All is woe and loss.

Route of Destruction

  1. Waded down the Hudson.
  2. Torched the financial district.
  3. Stepped on U.S. Naval attack ships, who were attempting to defend the city.
  4. Rolled over on large sections of Brooklyn.
  5. Then pranced back upstate, vanishing into the Adirondacks.
