Opie Rampages Through Tokyo.

Opie, the Destroyer of Worlds, began his reign of distruction in Tokyo. This debut attack was swift and brutal.

Statistics of Destruction

  • 10,000 structures were burned.
  • 400 bowls of Rammen were left uneaten durring the attack.
  • The attack lasted for 2 hours and 40 minutes.
  • Opie devoured the Tokyo Hard Rock Cafe.
  • 55 fighter jets and 25 tanks were destroyed trying to stop the rampage.
